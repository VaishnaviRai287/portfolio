### Email Phishing Detection Platform

A modern cybersecurity triage application designed to scan and scoring raw email envelopes for phishing vectors.

#### Key Features:
- **Envelope Parsing**: Ingests raw `.eml` files and decomposes them into headers, body components, and authentication records (SPF, DKIM, DMARC).
- **Security Telemetry**: Extracts link domains, IP geo-locations, and attachments to identify suspicious signatures.
- **Heuristic Risk Scoring**: Evaluates email risk using a weighted heuristic classifier.
